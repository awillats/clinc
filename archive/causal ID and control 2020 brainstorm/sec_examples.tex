\paragraph{Dynamics equations for various models}
PLDS, SISO:
\begin{flalign}
& x = \begin{bmatrix}
    x_A \\
    x_B \\
    x_C
    \end{bmatrix} &\\
& \dot{x} = Ax + Q\omega &\\
& y = Cx + \eta &\\
& z = \exp(y) &\\
& spk = \mathrm{Poiss}(z) & 
\end{flalign}

\hrulefill \\
PLDS, SISO w/Inputs:
\begin{flalign}
& x = \begin{bmatrix}
    x_A \\
    x_B \\
    x_C
    \end{bmatrix} &\\
& \dot{x} = Ax + Q\omega + Bu&\\
& y = Cx + \eta &\\
& z = \exp(y) &\\
& spk = \mathrm{Poiss}(z) & 
\end{flalign}
